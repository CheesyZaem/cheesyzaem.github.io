Candy Music

Candy Music is a music bot developed using Discord.JS library. It has several cool features in it, and of course more in the future. The bot is playing high quality music which can rarely be found in other music bots. I hope you enjoy using this music bot, and if you encounter any problems, feel free to ask for help in the support server - everything you need can be found from the help command.
▂ ▃ ▄ ▆ Features ▆ ▄ ▃ ▂

» High quality music.
» Loop feature.
» Allows up to 3 hours item from YouTube.
» YouTube playlist support.
» Fair skip, votes required to skip.
» Allows up to 100 items in queue at once.
» Customizable prefix.
» Constantly being updated.
» More incoming!
